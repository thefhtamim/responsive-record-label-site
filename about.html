Introducing you to a new music record label for those who don't have distribution deals with any record label/music distributor but want to stream and sell their music around the world on Spotify, Apple Music, TIDAL, Jio Saavn, Deezer, and more than 200+ stores!
Arteon Records is a part of the_fhtamim LLC. and powered by a industry certified Music Publishing Administration. Our vision is to support those artists who don't have enough money to pay a music distributor or enough recognition to work with big record labels.

What we will provide

  • Distribution Solutions

100% Free & No limits to the number of artists or releases, you can upload unlimited music through us. We have music distribution solutions for independent artists, labels, and collectives.

  • Marketing and promotion tools

Take your music even further with our unique promotional tools for your music and your fans.

  • Rights Protection

Earn notability from the usage of your music generated by users on YouTube for Sound Recordings and Composition, your Soundcloud profile, TikTok, Triller, and more while ensuring your copyrights are protected.

  • Artist Channel Verification

We will verify your accounts with a blue tick on Spotify, Youtube, and other possible social media platforms.
