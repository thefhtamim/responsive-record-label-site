Introducing you to a new music record label for those who don't have distribution deals with any record label/music distributor but want to stream and sell their music around the world on Spotify, Apple Music, TIDAL, Jio Saavn, Deezer, and more than 200+ stores!
Arteon Records is a part of the_fhtamim LLC. and powered by a industry certified Music Publishing Administration. Our vision is to support those artists who don't have enough money to pay a music distributor or enough recognition to work with big record labels.
